What You'll Need Before Starting
--------------------------------

Before visiting **activate.uhc.com**, gather the following:

- Your UHC Member ID (found on your insurance card or welcome letter)
- Full legal name, date of birth, and ZIP code
- A valid email address
- A secure password that meets UHC security requirements
- Your Social Security Number or ITIN
- Access to your mobile phone for verification codes

.. note::

   Personal information must match exactly what UHC has on file. Any mismatch
   may prevent successful activation.

Step-by-Step UHC Account Activation Process
-------------------------------------------

Follow these steps to activate your UHC account:

1. **Go to the Activation Portal:** Open your browser and visit
   ``https://activate.uhc.com``.
2. **Enter Member Information:** Input your UHC Member ID as it appears on your card.
3. **Verify Identity:** Provide your name, date of birth, and ZIP code.
4. **Create Login Credentials:** Choose a unique username and strong password.
5. **Set Up Security Questions:** Select and answer questions for account safety.
6. **Verify Your Email:** Check your inbox and confirm your email address.
7. **Complete Phone Verification:** Enter the SMS verification code sent to your phone.
8. **Accept Terms:** Review and agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
9. **Confirm Account Creation:** Review your details and submit.
10. **Access Your Dashboard:** Log in to explore your UHC online account.

How to Find Your UHC Member ID
-------------------------------

Your Member ID is required for activation. You can find it in:

- **Insurance Card:** Displayed prominently on your card.
- **Welcome Letter:** Included in your new member packet.
- **Enrollment Confirmation Email**
- **Employer HR Department:** If enrolled through workplace benefits.

.. note::

   If you cannot locate your ID, call UHC at **1-888-216-2896** for assistance.

Troubleshooting Common Activation Issues
----------------------------------------

Some users encounter challenges during activation. Here are solutions:

**Member ID Not Recognized**

- Re-enter exactly as shown on your card
- Ensure coverage has begun
- Remove extra spaces or characters
- Contact customer support if still not recognized

**Personal Information Mismatch**

- Ensure your name matches enrollment records
- Use the correct ZIP code associated with your file
- Check your date-of-birth format
- Verify no recent name changes are unprocessed

**Technical Difficulties**

- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Disable interfering extensions
- Try another browser or device
- Check your internet connection

Security Tips for UHC Account Management
----------------------------------------

To protect your healthcare information:
